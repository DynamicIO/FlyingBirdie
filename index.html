About Flying Birdie

Flying Birdie is an exciting arcade-style bird flying game that challenges your reflexes and timing. Navigate through obstacles, collect power-ups, and build combos to achieve the highest score possible. The game features a beautiful day/night cycle, particle effects, and responsive controls that work on both desktop and mobile devices.

Game Features:

  • Smooth gameplay with realistic physics
  • Power-ups: Slow Motion, Shield, and Double Points
  • Combo system for bonus points
  • Dynamic day/night cycle
  • Particle effects and visual feedback
  • High score tracking
  • Responsive design for all devices
  • Web Audio API sound effects

How to Play:

Control your bird by tapping the screen (mobile) or pressing spacebar/clicking (desktop). Navigate through the pipes without touching them. Collect power-ups to gain special abilities and build combos for higher scores. The game gets progressively more challenging as your score increases.
